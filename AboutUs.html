About Us

DinDin was created in the summer of 2022 to help pet owners organise their busy lives. In a world that's getting more hectic by the day, it can be easy to overlook life's routine details. Sometimes we need to be reminded to give our four-legged family members the nutrition and medication they need. With DinDin, you don't have to remember details. Let us worry about the little things so you can focus on your day.
